Cushioning: Lightweight EVA foam midsole absorbs shock waves.

Support: Side webbing for foot support.

Breathable: Breathable synthetic mesh allows air flow.

Lightweight: 242g in size 8½.

Age: Adult

Gender: Female

Composition: Mesh and synthetic upper injection-moulded EVA foam midsole, rubber outsole. .

Care Instructions: Do not machine wash; clean with a soft brush and mild soapy water; do not tumble dry.

Restriction of use: Not suitable for tennis and football.

What size should you buy?: For a running shoe you should normally buy a size bigger than your formal shoes keeping a space of approximately 1-1.5cms between your longest toe and front end of the shoe. During running foot tends to slip forward in the shoe and this space provides that room negating chances of front of the foot injuries.
