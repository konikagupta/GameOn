Why?: You are looking for a wireless cyclocomputer with basic functions and features that is easy to use.

Features: Speed partial distance total distance and time are calculated and displayed by this wireless cyclocomputer.

Easy to use: A manual is provided that depicts how you can use the cyclocomputer.

Easy assembly / dismantling: You need to clip the magnet on the spokes of your front tire. Ensure the sensor is attached to the front fork and is near the magent while the display is on the handlebar. No additional tools are required to setup the cyclocomputer.

Restriction of use: Should not be used under heavy rainfall. The Count 4 display unit is built to be water tight and resistant to rain with its casing but under heavy rainfall components such as the magnet and sensor could get damaged due to external factors.

Compatibility: The cyclocomputer can be used with all bikes
